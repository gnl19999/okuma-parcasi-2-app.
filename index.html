Green Turtles (A1 Reading)

Green turtles are very large. They live in the sea, and they like warm water.

These turtles eat green plants in the sea. They can grow to be 1-1,5 m long.

Bu kaplumbağalar denizde yeşil bitkileri yerler. 1-1,5 m uzunluğuna kadar büyüyebilirler.

The green turtle has a hard shell. The shell keeps the turtle safe.
