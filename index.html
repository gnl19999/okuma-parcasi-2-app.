Green turtles are big animals. They are a kind of sea turtle. Their name is "Green Turtle," but they are often brown or yellow. They live in the ocean.

Green turtles like warm water. They live in warm places like near beaches. They swim very fast. They use their strong legs (flippers) to swim.

A small green turtle is very small. But when they are adults, they are big! Some can be 1 meter long. They can live for a very long time, maybe 80 years!

What do they eat? When they are young, they eat small sea animals. But when they are big, they mainly eat plants in the sea, like seaweed. They are mostly herbivores.

Green turtles are important. We must help them. Many turtles are in danger. People must keep the beaches clean so turtles can make their nests and lay their eggs there.
